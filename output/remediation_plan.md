# Remediation Plan

## GAP-001 - KYC
- Status: Non-compliant
- Severity: High
- Required Policy: Section 4.2 - KYC Review Frequency
Financial institutions must perform KYC customer reviews every 12 months for high-risk customers.
- Current Policy: KYC review for high-risk customers is performed every 24 months
- Affected System: KYC Platform
- Priority: High
- Control Action: Update existing control to meet new regulatory requirement
- Owner: Compliance Team
- Target Deadline: 2026-08-02

## GAP-002 - Monitoring
- Status: Non-compliant
- Severity: High
- Required Policy: Section 5.1 - Suspicious Activity Monitoring
Institutions must monitor suspicious transactions daily and report high-risk activity within 48 hours.
- Current Policy: Suspicious transactions are reviewed weekly
- Affected System: AML Monitoring System
- Priority: High
- Control Action: Update existing control to meet new regulatory requirement
- Owner: AML Team
- Target Deadline: 2026-08-02

## GAP-003 - Audit
- Status: Non-compliant
- Severity: High
- Required Policy: Section 6.3 - Evidence Retention
Compliance teams must retain evidence and audit logs for at least 5 years.
- Current Policy: Evidence and audit logs are retained for 3 years
- Affected System: Document Management System
- Priority: High
- Control Action: Update existing control to meet new regulatory requirement
- Owner: Audit Team
- Target Deadline: 2026-08-02
