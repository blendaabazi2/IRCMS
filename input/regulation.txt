Regulatory Publication: AML Enforcement Directive 2026
Jurisdiction: EU
Regulator: European Banking Authority
Effective Date: 2026-07-01

Section 2.1 - Enhanced Due Diligence for High-Risk Clients
Financial institutions must complete enhanced due diligence for all high-risk clients within 30 days of onboarding. Failure to comply will result in penalties of up to EUR 10 million or 10% of annual turnover.

Section 3.4 - Real-Time Transaction Monitoring
Institutions must implement real-time transaction monitoring systems capable of flagging suspicious activity within 60 seconds. Non-compliance sanctions may reach EUR 5 million per breach.

Section 4.7 - Beneficial Ownership Verification
Institutions must verify and record beneficial ownership for all corporate clients within 14 days. Violations are subject to enforcement action and licence suspension.

Section 5.2 - Suspicious Activity Reporting
Compliance teams must file suspicious activity reports within 24 hours of detection. Breach of this requirement constitutes a regulatory violation liable to fines and remediation orders.

Section 6.1 - Staff Training and Certification
All compliance staff must complete AML certification training every 6 months. Non-compliance with training obligations may result in regulatory sanctions against the institution.
